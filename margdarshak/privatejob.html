Receptionist/Customer Service Representative

Some companies hire individuals with basic educational qualifications for front desk roles. As a receptionist or customer service representative, you may handle phone calls, greet visitors, and assist with basic administrative tasks. The salary for these positions can vary, but it is generally at an average level.

DATA ENTRY OPERATOR

Data entry jobs typically involve inputting information into computer systems or databases. These positions often require basic computer skills and a good typing speed. The salary for data entry operators can vary, but it is typically at the lower end of the pay scale.

DELIVER/COURIER SERVICES

Working as a delivery person or courier for companies like food delivery services, courier companies, or e-commerce platforms is another option. These jobs typically involve delivering goods to customers and may require a valid driver's license if driving is involved. The salary may be in the form of hourly wages or per delivery commissions.

Assistant in Small Businesses

Small businesses sometimes hire individuals for general assistant roles, which may involve tasks like filing, organizing inventory, or running errands. The salary for these positions can vary depending on the nature and size of the business.
